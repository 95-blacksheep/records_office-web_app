Purpose

The purpose of a police records office is to maintain accurate and comprehensive records of law enforcement activities and to provide support to police personnel and the public by providing access to these records.

Vision

The vision of a police records office is to be a leader in the management of law enforcement records and to be recognized for providing efficient and effective services that enhance the public's trust and confidence in the police department.

Mission

The mission of a police records office is to provide professional, timely, and accurate records management services that support the objectives of the police department. This includes maintaining complete and accurate records, ensuring the confidentiality and security of sensitive information, and providing prompt and courteous service to all customers. Additionally, the mission may include promoting public trust and transparency by making records accessible to the public in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
