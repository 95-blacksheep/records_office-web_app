Unlock the Power of Your Past, with the Convenience of Your Present

Welcome to the Police Records Office website. Here, you can access important information about police records and procedures. Our mission is to provide accurate and up-to-date records to the public in a timely and efficient manner. You can request records, report a crime, or find information about our services and policies. We strive to maintain the highest level of transparency and accountability in all of our operations. Thank you for visiting our website and we hope it serves as a helpful resource for you.

Accra Region

Local Police Department Launches Community Outreach Program The local police department has announced the launch of a new community outreach program aimed at strengthening the relationship between law enforcement and the community. The program, called "Bridging the Gap," will focus on increasing communication and collaboration between police officers and members of the community. The program will include a series of events and activities designed to educate the public on police procedures and build trust between the police and the community. Some of the events include community meetings, ride-alongs with police officers, and youth outreach initiatives.
